About the Computer Science Pathway

The computer science pathway at BASE is a collection of classes to teach you about the concepts of computer science. It has the following courses:

  • Tech Survey (9th)
  • AP Computer Science Principles (9/10th)
  • Cybersecurity (10/11th)
  • AP Computer Science A (11/12th)

I am currently taking AP CSP, and over the next two years I hope to take Cybersecurity and AP CSA.
