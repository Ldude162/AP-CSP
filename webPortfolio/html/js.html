BetterBetterMusic, a JavaScript project of mine

A ChatTriggers module for Hypixel Skyblock which lets you change the boss music in dungeons based off of the module BetterMusic, with features such as

  • Changing music in bosses, with a few different options
  • Changing volume of music
  • Turning off the music if you don't want it.
